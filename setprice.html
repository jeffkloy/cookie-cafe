Market Value

The "Market Value" of a thing is the price that somebody would pay for that thing in a competitive economy.

If the average cost of cookies of your competitors is $3.00, then $3.00 is the current market value.

Does Market Value change?

Yes! It changes according to how valueable the product is. Value is determined by scarcity.
